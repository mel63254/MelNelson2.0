About me specifically.

My birthplace

I was born in Luverne MN.

I grew up in Southwest Minnesota hunting, and fishing. I spent most summers outside with friends, returning home once the street lights came on. As I got older, I learned to appreciate them old dirt roads. They lead to new adventures, as well as new fishing holes. I quickly became familiar enough to navigate the whole corner of Minnesota without ever traveling on a surface road.

I moved to southeast South Dakota when I turned 20. I resided there only a very short time. This is where I began my career in logistics. It wasn't long before I realized South Dakota was no where near big enough for me. So once again I packed up, and headed out west.

At age 23 I moved to Portland, OR. I spent an amazing two years partying like my life depended on it. I met some amazing folks down there, and learned a bit more about life before moving up here to the greater Seattle area. I've been up here now just over 12 years. Just this last April I traveled to Thailand with my wife. Bangkok was amazing!! We have since talked about returning, sooner than later.
